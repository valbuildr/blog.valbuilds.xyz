I redesigned all of the platform signs in SCR because I was bored.

## Transport for Stepford / National Rail

There are 8 stations managed by National Rail, with half of them being with Transport for Stepford.

These signs feature the font [Instrument Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Instrument+Sans).

### The Transport for Stepford Logo

I've simply changed the font on the TfS logo to [Instrument Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Instrument+Sans). The icon hasn't been changed.
